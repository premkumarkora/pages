Artificial Intelligence: For the Decision Makers

Real-World Applications of AI

Through real-world examples, the book explores how AI is already driving innovation across industries. Learn how:

  • Predictive maintenance is saving manufacturers millions by preventing equipment failures.
  • Logistics companies optimize delivery routes with real-time traffic data.
  • Personalized product recommendations are boosting sales in retail.
  • In healthcare, AI is detecting diseases from medical images and predicting patient outcomes, enabling better, faster, and more accurate diagnoses.
